Our mark is the single most recognizable expression of the brand. Protect its form, its color, and its breathing room.

Reversed — on dark

Minimum Size

Never render the logo smaller than 120px wide on digital surfaces. Legibility is non-negotiable.

80px — not allowed
120px — minimum
200px — recommended

Clear Space

Keep a padding equal to the height of one star (X) on every side. No text, no imagery, no edges inside this zone.

06 — Voice

Voice & Tone

Authoritative. Trustworthy. Patriotic. We speak in English only, we speak in short sentences, and we speak with conviction.

Do

  • Write in English only, across every surface.
  • Use short, declarative sentences. Subject, verb, point.
  • Lead with conviction — state the stance, then the proof.
  • Speak as one national voice, not as an individual.
  • Prefer specific, measurable claims over abstractions.
  • Use active voice. Attribute actions to the movement.

Don't

  • Do not mix Urdu, Roman Urdu, or regional languages into copy.
  • Avoid casual language, slang, or internet shorthand.
  • No hedging — skip "maybe," "sort of," "we think."
  • Do not use exclamation marks to manufacture energy.
  • No empty adjectives: "amazing," "incredible," "world-class."
  • Do not borrow the tone of opponents or tabloid headlines.
Headline — bold, direct
Leading Pakistan Forward.
Subject. Direction. Full stop. No qualifiers.
Tagline — rhythmic, patriotic
One Nation. One Voice. One Future.
Three beats. Parallel structure. Works on a poster, a tee, or a banner.
Body copy — clear, factual

We will invest in every province. We will publish our budget in full. We will hold every commitment to a public deadline. That is the standard. That is the promise.

Short sentences. Active verbs. No fluff. Every claim is specific.
Buttons — action-oriented
Every label begins with a verb. Title case. No more than four words.
